20 Seconds To Launch

20 Seconds To Launch is a 2D game create in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2020. The theme of this game jam was "to fix".

The game involves repairing a rocket before it launches, in 20 seconds. It is based on the concept of die and retry, that is, the player must try again and again until they find the right actions to make the rocket take off.

  • Languages: Unity3D (C#)
  • Repository: tguichaoua/20-seconds-to-launch
Game Jam

Source: Wikipedia

A game jam is a contest where participants try to make a video game from scratch. Depending on the format, participants might work independently, or in teams. The contest duration usually ranges from 24 to 72 hours. Participants are generally programmers, game designers, artists, writers, and others in game development-related fields.
