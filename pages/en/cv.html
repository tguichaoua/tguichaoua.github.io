Laboratory research internship

European Center for Virtual Reality
31 January 2022 - 29 July 2022

This is my end-of-studies internship as part of my master's degree in Interactive, Intelligent and Autonomous Systems.

The subject was the design of a tracking and communication system for a swarm of robots. The goal being to increase their perception to be able to know the position of the neighbors. Ultimately, this project will aim the study of behavioral algorithms on the swarm of robots. Prior to this internship, I carried out a study bibliography which served me as a state of the art of existing localization methods.

The robots used were Mona robots. These robots are often used in the field of research and education because of their low cost and the fact that they are open-source and open-hardware.

The technical solution adopted is to use the OptiTrack system to obtain the position of the robots, then to pass this information to a server coded in Rust which has the role of reworking and redistributing this information to the robots.

Master M2 — Interactive, Intelligent and Autonomous Systems

2021 - 2022

The IIAS course highlights co-accreditation with the main Brest higher education institutions (UBO, ENIB, ENSTA Bretagne and IMT-Atlantique) in order to build a common diploma based on their cutting-edge knowledge in the fields of Intelligent and Autonomous Interactive Systems.

The main theme of this course focuses on computer systems linked to humans. More particularly, the notion of interactions between artificial systems and humans, whether it is when the human is immersed in a complex system, or when he conceives the complexity and creates this system.

The course focuses on current knowledge in artificial intelligence, learning, cognitive science, modeling and verification, virtual reality, robotics, sensor networks, modeling and simulation using multi-agent systems.
