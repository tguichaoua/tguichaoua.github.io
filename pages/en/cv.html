Master M2 — Interactive, Intelligent and Autonomous Systems

2021 - 2022

The IIAS course highlights co-accreditation with the main Brest higher education institutions (UBO, ENIB, ENSTA Bretagne and IMT-Atlantique) in order to build a common diploma based on their cutting-edge knowledge in the fields of Intelligent and Autonomous Interactive Systems.

The main theme of this course focuses on computer systems linked to humans. More particularly, the notion of interactions between artificial systems and humans, whether it is when the human is immersed in a complex system, or when he conceives the complexity and creates this system.

The course focuses on current knowledge in artificial intelligence, learning, cognitive science, modeling and verification, virtual reality, robotics, sensor networks, modeling and simulation using multi-agent systems.
